PU Palette compares colors in the CIE L*a*b* color space, using Delta E94 for comparative calcluations. While this results in fairly accurate results in line with how humans perceive color difference, there are still some inaccuracies in the Delta E94 itself, typically falling short when calculating the perceived lightness of colors and with some blue variations.
